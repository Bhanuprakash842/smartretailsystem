# Inventory Management & Stock Policies

## Stock Level Definitions
- **In Stock**: Product is available and ready for immediate dispatch.
- **Low Stock**: Product has fewer than 10 units remaining. Dashboard alerts are triggered for admins.
- **Out of Stock**: Product is currently unavailable. Customers can sign up for "Back in Stock" notifications.
- **Pre-order**: New product launches that can be reserved before the official release date.

## Replenishment Cycle
Our inventory is reviewed daily based on the AI-powered demand forecasting models (GradientBoosting and Random Forest). 
- **Electronics**: Weekly restock cycle.
- **Fashion**: Seasonal restock cycle.
- **Home Decor**: Bi-weekly restock cycle.

## Quality Control
All products undergo a 3-point quality check before being added to the stock:
1. Packaging integrity check.
2. Functional verification (for electronics).
3. Aesthetic inspection (for fashion and decor).

## Warehouse Locations
LuxeCart operates from three primary fulfillment centers:
- **North Zone**: Delhi NCR (Primary hub for Electronics)
- **West Zone**: Mumbai (Primary hub for Fashion)
- **South Zone**: Bangalore (Primary hub for Tech & Wearables)
