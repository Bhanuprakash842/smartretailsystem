About LuxeCart — Premium E-Commerce Platform
===============================================

Company Overview:
LuxeCart is a premium e-commerce platform dedicated to curating high-quality products
across Electronics, Wearables, Fashion, and Home Decor. Our mission is to make luxury
and quality accessible to everyone through a seamless digital shopping experience.

Founded: 2024
Headquarters: Hyderabad, India & San Francisco, USA
Team Size: 50+ passionate professionals

Our Values:
- Quality First: Every product on LuxeCart is vetted for quality and durability.
- Customer-Centric: We prioritize your satisfaction with easy returns, fast shipping,
  and responsive support.
- Innovation: From AI-powered demand forecasting to RAG-based customer support, we
  leverage cutting-edge technology to enhance your shopping experience.
- Sustainability: We use eco-friendly packaging and partner with brands committed to
  ethical manufacturing.

Technology Stack:
- Backend: Python Flask with SQLAlchemy ORM
- Database: Azure SQL (production), SQLite (development)
- AI/ML: Scikit-learn for demand forecasting, Pinecone for vector search,
  OpenAI for embeddings and natural language understanding
- Frontend: HTML5, TailwindCSS, JavaScript

Key Features:
1. Curated Product Catalog with detailed descriptions and high-quality imagery
2. Secure Checkout with multiple payment options
3. Real-time Order Tracking
4. AI-Powered Demand Forecasting for inventory optimization
5. RAG-Based QA Assistant for instant customer support
6. Admin Dashboard with analytics, order management, and product CRUD
