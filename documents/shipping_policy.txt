LuxeCart Shipping Policy
========================

Domestic Shipping (within India / US):
- Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days. Free on orders over ₹50 (or ₹3,500).
  Orders under ₹50 incur a flat ₹5.99 shipping fee.
- Express Shipping: 2–3 business days. Flat rate of ₹12.99 (₹999).
- Same-Day Delivery: Available in select metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore,
  Hyderabad, New York, Los Angeles, Chicago). Order before 12 PM local time.
  Flat rate of ₹19.99 (₹1,499).

International Shipping:
- We ship to over 40 countries worldwide.
- Standard International: 10–15 business days. Starting from ₹19.99.
- Express International: 5–7 business days. Starting from ₹34.99.
- Import duties, taxes, and customs clearance fees are the responsibility of the
  buyer and are not included in the shipping cost.

Order Tracking:
- All orders include a tracking number, sent via email within 24 hours of dispatch.
- Track your order at luxecart.com/track or through the carrier's website.

Shipping Restrictions:
- We do not ship to PO Boxes for Express or Same-Day orders.
- Certain products (e.g., items with lithium batteries like headphones and earbuds)
  may have restrictions for air freight to specific countries.

Packaging:
- All items are packed in eco-friendly, recyclable packaging.
- Fragile items (Ceramic Vase Set, Minimalist Lamp) receive extra bubble wrap and
  protective inserts.
- Gift wrapping is available for ₹3.99 per item.

Lost or Damaged Shipments:
- If your package is lost or arrives damaged, contact support@luxecart.com within
  7 days of the expected delivery date.
- We will issue a full refund or reship the item at no extra cost.
