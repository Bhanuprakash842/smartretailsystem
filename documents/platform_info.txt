# LuxeCart Platform & Team Information

## About LuxeCart
LuxeCart is a premium e-commerce platform dedicated to bringing high-quality electronics, fashion, and home decor to discerning customers. Founded in 2024, our mission is to combine luxury with cutting-edge technology.

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: HTML5, Vanilla CSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript.
- **Backend**: Python (Flask).
- **Database**: Azure SQL (MSSQL) with SQLAlchemy ORM.
- **AI/ML**: Demand forecasting using GradientBoosting and Random Forest models.
- **Chatbot**: RAG system powered by Google Gemini (LLM) and Pinecone (Vector Database).

## Our AI Vision
We leverage machine learning not just for recommendations, but for operational excellence. Our demand forecasting system predicts future sales with over 85% accuracy, allowing us to minimize waste and ensure products are always available when you need them.

## Sustainability Commitment
LuxeCart is committed to reducing our carbon footprint. 
- 80% of our packaging is biodegradable.
- We prioritize suppliers who follow ethical labor practices.
- Our delivery routes are optimized by AI to reduce fuel consumption.
