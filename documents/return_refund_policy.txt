LuxeCart Return & Refund Policy
================================

Return Window:
- All products can be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
- Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags/accessories intact.
- Ceramic Vase Set and Minimalist Lamp have a 15-day return window due to fragile nature.

How to Initiate a Return:
1. Go to luxecart.com/orders or navigate to "My Orders" from your account.
2. Select the order and click "Request Return."
3. Choose a reason for return and submit.
4. You will receive a prepaid return shipping label via email within 24 hours.
5. Pack the item securely and drop it off at the nearest carrier location.

Refund Process:
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item.
- The refund is credited back to the original payment method.
- For COD orders, refunds are issued to the bank account provided during the return request.

Non-Returnable Items:
- Products purchased during flash sales marked as "Final Sale."
- Items that have been used, damaged by the customer, or are missing accessories.
- Gift cards.

Exchange Policy:
- Direct exchanges are available for size/color variants (Fashion items only).
- For other exchanges, return the original item and place a new order.

Warranty Claims:
- Warranty claims are separate from returns. Contact support@luxecart.com with your
  order number and a description/photos of the issue.
- Warranty replacements are shipped free of charge.

Customer Support:
- Email: support@luxecart.com
- Live Chat: Available on the website Mon–Sat, 9 AM – 9 PM IST.
- Phone: +91 1800-LUXE-CART (toll-free) or +1 (800) 589-3227.
- Response time: Within 4 hours during business hours.
