LuxeCart — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
=============================================

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Click "Register" in the top navigation bar. Fill in your username, email, and
   password. You'll be able to start shopping immediately after registration.

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Browse our products on the homepage, click "Add to Cart" on any item, then go to
   your Cart and click "Proceed to Checkout." Enter your shipping address and payment
   method, then confirm your order.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Debit Cards, UPI, Net Banking,
   Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe), and Cash on Delivery (COD) for orders under ₹200.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Yes, you can cancel an order within 2 hours of placing it, as long as it hasn't
   been shipped. Go to "My Orders," find the order, and click "Cancel."

Q: How do I track my order?
A: After your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also
   track it from "My Orders" on the website.

Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Absolutely. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. We do not
   store your card details on our servers.

Q: Do you offer gift cards?
A: Yes! LuxeCart gift cards are available in ₹25, ₹50, ₹100, and ₹200 denominations.
   They can be purchased from the "Gift Cards" section and are delivered digitally
   via email.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach us via:
   - Email: support@luxecart.com
   - Live Chat: Mon–Sat, 9 AM – 9 PM IST
   - Phone: +91 1800-LUXE-CART (toll-free) or +1 (800) 589-3227

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, we ship to over 40 countries. International shipping takes 10–15 business
   days for standard and 5–7 for express. Customs duties are the buyer's responsibility.

Q: What is your return policy?
A: Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery in unused condition.
   See our full Return & Refund Policy for details.

Q: How does the demand forecasting feature work?
A: Our AI-powered demand forecasting uses machine learning models (Gradient Boosting
   and Random Forest) trained on historical sales data. The admin dashboard shows
   predicted demand for each product, helping with inventory planning and promotional
   strategy.

Q: What is the RAG QA Assistant?
A: The RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) QA Assistant is an AI chatbot that answers
   questions about LuxeCart products, policies, and services. It retrieves relevant
   information from our knowledge base and generates accurate, context-aware responses.
   You can access it via the floating chat icon on any page.
